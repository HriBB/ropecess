# Ropecess

Marketing site for Ropecess, a rope access and specialized construction company. Bilingual (English default, Slovenian under `/sl`), with inquiry forms that email the company.

## Language

**Inquiry**:
A message from a prospective client sent via a site form, delivered to the company as email. Kinds: Contact Inquiry, Spacenet Inquiry.
_Avoid_: message, lead, submission

**Service**:
Work Ropecess performs on a client's structure or site. Currently: photovoltaic module installation, industrial rope access, rigging, stage building, professional height cleaning.
_Avoid_: offering, solution

**Professional Height Cleaning**:
A Service promoted standalone at the request of Ropecess's owner — has its own page, menu item, and homepage section, unlike other Services.
_Avoid_: window cleaning (a sub-service), cleaning

**Work Plan**:
The project phase where scope, timeline, budget, and resources are planned before work begins. A step in every project, not a Service.

**Access Plan**:
The project phase where the method for safely reaching the work area (rope access, rigging) is designed. Follows the Work Plan; not a Service.

**Spacenet**:
A custom suspended net installation built for events and festivals — a product line Ropecess builds, distinct from a Service.
_Avoid_: space net, net service

**Design Variant**:
One of five complete site designs (all pages, both locales, own header/footer, light+dark) built so the client can pick the site's next look. Identified by key and name (a — Airy Organic, b — Exaggerated Minimal, c — Editorial Calm, d — Neo-Brutalist, e — Aurora Glass).
_Avoid_: theme (collides with light/dark), prototype (the variants outlive the homepage prototype), mockup

**Design Preview**:
The client-facing, token-gated area of the production site where all Design Variants are browsable. Unguessable URL, excluded from search indexing, removable by unsetting its token.
_Avoid_: staging, demo site, test site
